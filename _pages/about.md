 I am currently a research student enrolled in the 3+5 Qiangji Program. I completed my undergraduate studies at the [School of Physics, Nankai University](https://physics.nankai.edu.cn), and am now conducting research at the [College of Computer Science, Nankai University](https://cc.nankai.edu.cn/). My research is carried out in the Media Computing Lab, affiliated with the Tianjin Key Laboratory of Visual Computing and Intelligent Perception (VCIP).
I am fortunate to be advised by [Prof.Mingming Cheng](https://mmcheng.net/cmm/comment-page-1/) and [Prof.Xialei Liu](https://mmcheng.net/xliu/). My primary research interests focus on continual learning, visual token pruning in Vision-Language Models (VLMs), and inference acceleration. I am dedicated to exploring efficient and lightweight methods for visual-language intelligence, with the goal of enhancing the performance and deployment efficiency of VLMs in real-world scenarios.
